EMS (Electro Muscle Stimulation) works by sending low-frequency electrical impulses through a specialized suit to activate up to 90% of your muscle fibers simultaneously. This is far more than the 30% typically engaged during conventional exercise. Combined with guided bodyweight exercises, the result is a highly efficient full-body workout in just 20 minutes.
